Jumbo Knotless Braids

The Jumbo braiding style comprises 6 rows extending across the forehead from ear to ear, featuring up to 22 braids. With a longevity of 3-5 weeks, this style offers a quick and convenient option for those seeking a swift yet stylish solution.

Large Knotless Braids

The Large Braids style is an ideal choice for those seeking a simple yet elegant look. It's particularly well-suited for vacations, boasting a relatively short time in the chair. Customization options are available, including a butt-length add-on for additional length, a personalized color blend for those looking to explore new styles, or curls with flexi rods for added versatility. This style features 8 rows across the forehead, totaling about 35 braids, and has a longevity of 4-6 weeks.

Medium Braids

The Medium Knotless braids are our most popular style, perfect for everyday wear. With a manageable length and ideal size, this style offers a comfortable sitting experience.Customize your look with add-ons like a butt-length extension for extra length, a personalized color blend for a bold change, or curls with flexi rods. Featuring 8 rows across the forehead (approximately 48 braids), this style has a lasting period of 4-8 weeks. Notably, it's Shonda's favorite size to work with!

Smedium Braids

The Smedium style encompasses 10 rows extending across the forehead from ear to ear, incorporating approximately 75 braids. This style offers a longevity of 6-8 weeks. For individuals with longer natural hair, kindly consider selecting the "Long Hair" add-on, as this requires additional time for completion.

Small Braids

The Small Knotless braiding technique is a popular choice, offering a sleek appearance and extended durability. This style, consisting of 12 rows and approximately 95 braids, provides customization options such as a butt-length add-on, a personalized color blend, or curls with flexi rods. With a longevity of 7-10 weeks, this style requires 7-8 hours for completion.
